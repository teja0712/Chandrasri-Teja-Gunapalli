Project Overview

This project involved creating a customer segmentation model to identify high-value customers. The insights led to a 15% increase in targeted marketing efficiency.

What You Can See:

  • Customer segments based on purchasing behavior (e.g., high-value, medium-value, low-value).
  • Demographic information for each segment (e.g., age, location, gender).
  • Customer lifetime value (CLV) and churn risk analysis.
  • Product preferences for each segment.

Actions You Can Perform:

  • Filter customers by segment, location, or purchase history.
  • View detailed profiles of individual customers.
  • Export segmentation data for use in marketing campaigns.
  • Compare segments side-by-side for better decision-making.
