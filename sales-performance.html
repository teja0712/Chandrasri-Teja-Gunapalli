Project Overview

This dashboard tracks sales performance across regions, providing insights into revenue, growth, and key metrics. It was developed using Power BI and integrates data from multiple sources.

What You Can See:

  • Total sales revenue by region and product category.
  • Monthly sales trends and year-over-year growth.
  • Top-performing products and regions.
  • Sales targets vs. actual performance.

Actions You Can Perform:

  • Filter data by region, product, or time period.
  • Drill down into specific regions or products for detailed insights.
  • Export data to Excel for further analysis.
  • Switch between different visualizations (e.g., bar charts, line charts).
