Project Overview

This financial reporting dashboard provides real-time tracking of revenue and expenses. It improved reporting efficiency by 30%.

What You Can See:

  • Total revenue, expenses, and profit margins.
  • Monthly and quarterly financial performance.
  • Breakdown of expenses by category (e.g., marketing, operations).
  • Cash flow analysis and forecasts.

Actions You Can Perform:

  • Filter data by time period, department, or expense category.
  • Drill down into specific transactions or expense items.
  • Export financial reports in PDF or Excel format.
  • Set up alerts for unusual spending patterns.
